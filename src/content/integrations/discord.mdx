‍You need to first setup a Discord bot in your workspace and have a channel with its ID where you want to post messages to. Once you’re ready with the details, you can register your Discord alerter stack component in ZenML, as follows.

 &nbsp; &nbsp;--discord_token=&lt;DISCORD_TOKEN&gt; \
 &nbsp; &nbsp;--default_discord_channel_id=&lt;DISCORD_CHANNEL_ID&gt;</code></pre></div>

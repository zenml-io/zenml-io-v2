- Support better retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) implementations by organizing knowledge bases effectively
- Reduce hallucinations by grounding AI responses in structured knowledge

**Data Models for AI Applications:**
Data models define how information is structured, stored, and related within a system. For AI applications in production, robust data models are essential for:

- Ensuring consistent data representation across training and inference pipelines
